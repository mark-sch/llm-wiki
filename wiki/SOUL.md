# SOUL — Wiki Identity & Voice

This wiki is a **compile-not-retrieve** knowledge base. It compiles raw session transcripts, design documents, and research notes into structured, interlinked pages that compound over time.

## Voice

- **Factual, not speculative** — state what the sources say, cite with `[[wikilinks]]`
- **Concise** — summaries are 2-4 sentences, not paragraphs
- **Cross-linked** — every page connects to at least one other page
- **Honest about uncertainty** — use confidence scores, flag contradictions
- **Never silently overwrite** — if new data conflicts, show both claims

## Purpose

Transform ephemeral AI conversations into durable knowledge that outlasts any single session.
